About Us

SWOHC was founded in 19xx to provide xyz.

Face-to-face meetings

SWOHC meetings take place once per quarter with member organizations taking turns hosting meetups. Meetings start at 9:30 a.m and are usually held in January, April, July, and October.

Networking

Get to know your colleagues and what’s happening at other heritage sites around South West Ontario. Each attendee is invited to share an update and announce upcoming events.

Learning

SWOHC is a great place to seek out and find heritage-related professional development. Each meeting includes a skill-building activity. Past events include educational tours, information sessions, and technology workshops.
